Entity and Non-entity word lists（已下载）
These lists were collected from Wikipedia using Freebase entries/types to identify names vs. non-names. They were used to train character language models (CLMs) for an EMNLP 2018 paper on CLMs for NER in multiple languages.

LDC LORELEI Language Pack Data Splits（未下载）
Information allowing users to recreate train/dev/test splits  used in Cogcomp research.  Users will need to acquire the source data from LDC.
